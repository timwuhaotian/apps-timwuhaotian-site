## Design Context

### Users
App Store reviewers, app users, and support visitors who need stable public
intro, privacy, terms, and support URLs for Tim Wu Haotian's apps.

### Brand Personality
Calm, precise, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a quiet operational
directory: clear enough for policy review, polished enough to represent the app
portfolio, and restrained enough that the legal/support content stays legible.

### Aesthetic Direction
Soft editorial utility with warm tinted neutrals, app-specific accent colors,
rounded product surfaces, and browser-directory references. Avoid loud marketing
hero treatment, generic SaaS gradients, and decorative effects that distract
from app facts and policy links.

### Design Principles
Use one source of truth for app facts and let the UI make those facts scannable.
Keep spacing rhythmic and generous without hiding the directory workflow.
Make every action obvious, keyboard-accessible, and touch-friendly.
Use app accents sparingly to identify products, not to dominate the page.
Preserve legal and support copy clarity over ornamental language.
